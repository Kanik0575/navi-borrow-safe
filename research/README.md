## Research Ethics

- **Participant consent**: All 8 interviewees gave verbal consent for anonymized use of their responses in this product research. No names, employers, or identifying details are published.
- **Full transcripts**: Session notes were taken in Notion during each interview. Notes are not published here to protect participant privacy. The synthesis document captures all product-relevant themes.
- **Play Store data**: Scraped via `google-play-scraper` (open source, public data). Reviews are public content.
- **Secondary sources**: All financial and market data citations are in-line in each document.
